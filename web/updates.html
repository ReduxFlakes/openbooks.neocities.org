OpenBooks 8 Series

Version Numbering:

The first digit is the major version, 8 for OpenBooks 8 Series. The second is the Feature Version, used for global changes and new features being added or removed. The third is for content updates, such as changes to page text, blog posts, or OpenBooks News.
