Site Version 5.0

August 5th, 2016

Welcome to OpenBooks 5.0. Here's a quick rundown of the new features:

  • Search - A quick and easy way to search Neocities sites.
  • Directory - Hand-picked Neocities sites, sorted into categories. For now, just a select few Neocities sites have been added, but more will come soon.
  • Lounge - the Discord server for Neocities users.
  • Promote - Coming Soon.

OpenBooks 5.0 is a major overhaul, and the entire site has been rebuilt from the ground up with modern web technologies. The design language has also changed drastically, with a new, lighter theme and fonts optimized for web content.
