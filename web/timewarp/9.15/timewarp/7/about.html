About this site

First created in September 2014, Openbooks has taken many forms over the years, from a basic Geocities inspired site, to a Google-esque Neocities search giant, and back to a personal page. Its current form is inspired by its earlier days, and takes a much simpler approach to web design with less regard to what it "right" and "wrong" and "good" design.

This site is created with Dreamweaver CS6, and sometimes Dreamweaver MX. I use macOS, on various computers including but not limited to: A 2015 5k iMac, a 2011 MacBook Pro, a PowerBook G4, and several iBooks.
