OPP Beta Terms

Please read these carefully before applying for OPP Beta, and before including embeddable banners on your website.

OpenBooks Promotion Platform is not advertising. Only Neocities sites can be displayed, no products or services will be advertised. To feature your site, you must provide its name, URL, and a short description. Banners are not updated automatically, if your site undergoes changes, the URL changes, or anything else, contact marinexley@protonmail.com. The same goes for removing your site from the OPP. If your site goes down, as in, returns a 404 error, it will be removed.
