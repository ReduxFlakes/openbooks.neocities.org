Promote other Neocities users with easily embeddable buttons. Your own site can be featured by clicking the apply link above.

Information

OPP banners come in 3 sizes, which can be embedded into your own Neocities site. The banners are dynamic and refresh every 30 seconds.

Buttons

88x31 pixel web buttons. Takes up less space on your site, and fits in with other web buttons.

Code:

<iframe src="http://openbooks.neocities.org/sites/opp/system/8831.html" height="31" width="88" scrolling="no" frameborder="0"></iframe>

Banners

Banners are available in two sizes, a smaller 468 pixels wide version and a larger 768 pixels wide version.

Code:

<iframe src="http://openbooks.neocities.org/sites/opp/system/46860.html" height="60" width="468" scrolling="no" frameborder="0"></iframe>

Code:

<iframe src="http://openbooks.neocities.org/sites/opp/system/72890.html" height="90" width="728" scrolling="no" frameborder="0"></iframe>
