Welcome to Articles on OpenBooks
By Marin Exley November 5th, 2015.

Welcome to Articles Beta. OpenBooks Articles will feature editorials, reviews, and much more - all available for free on chromebooks.neocities.org. Right now, Articles is in beta, but in the coming months, anyone will be able to submit an article to be published on the OpenBooks Article Archive.

How do I submit an article?

Simply submit an article to Marin Exley via email - my email is all over the site, you can't miss it. You can use your own HTML and CSS styles to design your article exactly the way you want it, or you can use the standard template, like I'm using now.

What kind of things can I write?

Almost anything can be accepted into the OpenBooks Article Archive, however ChromeBooks is mostly focused on vintage technology. Reviews of technology new and old are always appreciated. Guides, editorials, opinions - almost anything can be published. If you're not sure, just email me with your idea.

Thank you for your interest in OpenBooks Articles.

- Marin
