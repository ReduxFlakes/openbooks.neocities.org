Compatibility

OpenBooks is designed to work in any modern browser, however there are a few recommendations:

Image rendering and blur effects work best in a WebKit (Safari, GNOME Web) or Blink (Chrome, Edge, Opera) web browser. Firefox is not recommended.

On mobile: Safari is recommended.

Javascript is recommended but not required.

A screen resolution of 1024x768 (XGA) is recommended, with sRGB coverage. High DPI/Retina display is supported.

A home on the web.

OpenBooks was started in September 2014 by Coyote Reyne, as a personal hub for their projects. It has evolved over time, sometimes with more of a focus on providing services to the Neocities community, and sometimes being a more personal site.

In its current form, OpenBooks exists as a hub for other Neocities projects, as well as a select few services, such as a search engine, and WebRing. It also hosts several projects by The OpenBooks Authors and Coyote Reyne.
